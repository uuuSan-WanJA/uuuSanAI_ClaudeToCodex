## Question

How should delegated scan or verification results be structured so the main converter can integrate them without losing evidence quality?

## Scope

- scan-unit outputs
- verification-unit outputs
- fact vs interpretation separation
- evidence-bearing summaries

## Current Direction

Use a fixed reporting protocol that separates context, structure, interpretation, and optional persona commentary.
